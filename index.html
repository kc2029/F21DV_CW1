we can see that poorer African countries have the lowest vaccine rate and death, this is most likely due to a lack of proper equipment and means to keep a record of covid, rather than any indication of their effectiveness of covid response,

Asian countries, on the other hand, has much lower death per million compares to European countries with similar vaccination rates. This is likely due to Asian country has much stricter lockdown rules and its population are far more likely to follow the rules than European counterpart
